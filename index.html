Our Story

NLM Clothing is the official apparel brand of NLM Music Group Africa, founded in 2025 by Ailwei Maduna Wa Malada in honor of his mother, Ntampose Lettia Maduna. Our clothing line represents the fusion of African musical heritage with contemporary fashion, creating unique pieces that allow fans to wear their passion for music. Each item is designed with quality materials and attention to detail, reflecting the same dedication to excellence that defines NLM Music Group Africa.
